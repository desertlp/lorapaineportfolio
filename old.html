I'm a front-end developer who loves creating beautiful and functional websites. I've been tinkering with web development for years, and I'm always excited to learn new things and stay up-to-date with the latest technologies.

My approach to development is all about finding the perfect balance between form and function. I believe that a great website not only looks good, but it also needs to be simple, easy to use and navigate. I'm passionate about creating user-friendly interfaces that make it easy for users to find what they're looking for.

My experience in sales has given me a unique perspective on customer needs and behavior. This, combined with my friendly and approachable nature, makes me an ideal candidate for working cross-functionally between teams. I understand the importance of effective communication and collaboration, and I know how to bridge the gap between technical teams and non-technical stakeholders.

Whether I'm working with designers, developers, or business stakeholders, I always prioritize building strong relationships and fostering a positive team culture. I believe that a happy and motivated team is essential for producing high-quality work that meets the needs of our users and stakeholders.
